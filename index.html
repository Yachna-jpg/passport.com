Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Simply upload your preferred image or design on the product page, select your size and material, and proceed to checkout. Our team reviews every order within 24 hours and will reach out if any clarification is needed.

Standard custom orders are delivered within 1–2 weeks depending on your location and the complexity of the design. Express delivery options are available at checkout for an additional fee.

We accept Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery for orders within India. International orders can be paid via PayPal or Stripe.

Custom printed covers cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. In such cases, we offer a free replacement within 7 days of delivery. Standard items can be exchanged within 14 days.
